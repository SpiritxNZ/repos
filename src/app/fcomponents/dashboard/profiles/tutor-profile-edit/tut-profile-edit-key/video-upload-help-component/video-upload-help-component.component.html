You can embed your video in the following steps:


Step 1. In YouTube, find the video that you want to link


Step 2. Below the video area, click Share



Step 3. In the pop-up window, click 'Embed'



Step 4. From the pop-up box, copy the code.



Step 5. Return to the Learnspace Tutor's profile page, paste the code and submit, your video has been linked!
